Do Not Steal This Repository

This repository is intentionally empty.

It exists as a symbolic protest against the use of human-created work for AI training without permission, attribution, transparency, or compensation.

There is no code here. That is the point.

Why this exists

When creators lose control over how their work is copied, absorbed, and reused, withholding the work becomes a form of speech.

Principles

Consent. Credit. Transparency. Compensation.
